Know the rules

Even if we play for fun, it is necessary to respect certain rules in order to guarantee a pleasant gaming experience.

1

Carkill

Carkill is not allowed, however if it happens, let the person reposition himself.

2

Boost

It is forbidden to be killed in order to increase the score of another player.

3

Glitch

It is obviously forbidden to take advantage of any game bug.

4

Fairplay

Never forget that you are playing for fun, so try to stay fairplay at all times.

In the event of non-compliance with the regulations, sanctions will be taken, going from a simple warning to final banishment.

Understand the ranking system

Each player's ranking is instantly updated each time he kills or is killed by another player. Players are split in 6 divisions according to their number of points.

TOP 10

This division brings together the 10 best players.

DIAMOND

This division brings together the 4% best players.

PLATINIUM

This division brings together the 8% best players.

Calculation of the number of points

The number of points won and lost depends on the ranking of the two players according to the following algorithm:

newRank = yourRank +  (rankPlayerKilled / yourRank * 5)

Decrease for inactivity

The ranking decreases automatically if the player has not made a kill during the day. This reduction is made according to the division:

Bronze
0 points
Silver
1 points
Gold
2 points
Platinium
3 points
Diamond
5 points
TOP 10
10 points

Reduction for distant kills

Kills made over 50 meters earn less points proportionally to the distance according to the following algorithm:

earnedPoints = normalKillPoints * (50 / distance)

Rank initialization and reset

Rank is initialised at 1000, which corresponds to the Gold division. Rank is reset to 1000 each month. The highest ranked player wins a VIP and a Top 1 skin.

It is also possible to reset your ranking to 1000 by being VIP and going to tour profile.
