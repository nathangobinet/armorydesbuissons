An Arma 3 server

Armory des buissons is a training server for the game Arma 3. It offers many features such as ranked PVP, public and private arenas, sandbox and attack-defense fight.

Go ahead and join us now to become the best player.

Connect

Discover the features

Be teleported directly into the fight

The game panel (Y) allows you to teleport directly to different combat zones. You can also activate automatic teleportation during respawn. The game panel also tells you the number of players in each area.

Choose your gameplay

Different types of figting zone are available

Long range fight

You can train in long distance combat thanks to the large arenas with an open field.

Close range fight

You can train in short distance combat thanks to the small arenas like towns or factory.

Fight in versus

Invite your friends

Invite your friends and split up into two teams.

Set the points limit

Define the number of kills to reach to win the game.

Choose your arena

A variety of short or long distance arenas are available.

Get the highest ranking

Each player's ranking is instantly updated each time he kills or is killed by another player. Players are split in 6 divisions according to their number of points.

Attack or defend the bomb

A public lobby system

It allows you to sign up for the fight and choose your side.

Defenders must defend the bomb

Their goal is to prevent attackers from defusing it.

Multiple arenas are available

Arenas are varied and offer a different experience.

Attackers must defuse the bomb

Their goal is to prevent the bomb from exploding and therefore defuse it

And even more to discover

Sandbox

Sandbox allows you to try out all the vehicles available on the game. Train yourself to fly helicopters and fight against your friends in epic battles.

Inventory

You can completely customize your stuff. Outfit, weapons, accessory ... Create the stuff that suits you and fight with it.

Gameplay

The server is perfectly programmed to offer you the best gameplay, whether you are alone or with others.
